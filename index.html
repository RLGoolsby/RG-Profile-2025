About me

My journey to becoming a Frontend Web Developer has been anything but straightforward. It has been a winding path that led me to this virtual career. I asked myself one day, "What next"? I was looking for a new challenge, something that would allow me to be creative. while satisfying my curiosity to take a design concept and bring it to life. I found that challenge in web development.
